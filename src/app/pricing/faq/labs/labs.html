Frequently Asked Questions

What should we consider as a lab?
A lab consists of research lab members, most often headed by a principal investigator.
Is there a setup fee?
No. There is no setup fee.
Do lab members have to pay to join the lab profile and manage their profile?
Absolutely not. Payment is made just for the lab profile itself.
Do I need a web host?
Academig offers you secure and unlimited web hosting.
What are the rates for publishing and managing my lab open positions?
You don’t have to pay any extra to manage your open positions and there are no hidden costs.
Do I have to enter my credit card information to build my lab profile?
No, you can sign up and build you lab profile without providing your credit card details.
Can I cancel at any time?
Yes, you can easily cancel and delete your lab profile at any time.
How long is the contract period?
All Academig rates run from month to month, unless you choose a yearly plan.
Do you offer tariffs with discounts?
Yes, we offer a discount on the annual plans as well as a discount if you need to manage multiple lab profiles.
Can I change my tariff later?
Definitely! You can extend or downgrade your plan at any time.
Are Academig payments secure?
Yes. Academig is Level 1 PCI DSS compliant certified. This means that all your transactions are ultra-secure.
