Lab interview

We offer you to conduct an interview with us, written or podcast style, as you may choose. The interview will appear in your lab profile and in our daily newsletter called Academig Reading. An interview can help your lab profile visitors to learn much more about your lab.

Academig cake club

What’s Academig Cake Club? Glad you asked. We love cakes at Academig and thought it’d be nice if we could share that with the research labs we work with. Academig Cake Club is our metaphorical way of accomplishing that. Our goal is to help every lab that join Academig, and serve every single lab a cake and a cup of tea to one of your next lab meetings, along with other great perks along the way.
