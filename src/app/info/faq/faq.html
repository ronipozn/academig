What is Academig?

Academig is the world’s 1st platform dedicated solely to finding and listing research labs. Academig launched publicly in 2018.

What should we consider as a lab?

A lab consists of research lab members, most often headed by a principal investigator.

To post a job on Academig:

  1. Head to Academig post job page.
  2. Fill out the job and lab information forms.
    If you’ve posted with Academig before, you can add a new job from your lab profile.
  3. Preview your job posting, fill out payment information and press “Post job and place your order”. No other sign up or login is required.
  4. A receipt and instructions on how to edit your lab profile and job posting will be sent to the email provided immediately after successful payment.

Are Academig payments secure?

All payments are processed securely by Stripe.

How long do my job listings stay live?

Listings stay live until the job vacancy is filled if no deadline specifed, or until the deadline if one does exist.
We send reminders about the status of your posting.

Is there a setup fee for the lab profile?

There is a setup fee for the lab profile only if you choose the Professional Lab Profile feature.

Do I need a web host?

Academig offers you secure and unlimited web hosting.

Do lab members have to pay to join a lab profile?

Absolutely not. Payment is made just for the lab profile itself and for job postings.

What does it mean to manage lab profile content?

You optimise your listing by updating your lab description, adding services, and highlighting your publications and collaborations to attract attention.

Do I have to enter my credit card information to build my lab profile?

No, you can sign up and build you lab profile without providing your credit card details. Only if and when you switch to LAB PRO or LAB PRO+ plans, you will be asked for your credit card details.
