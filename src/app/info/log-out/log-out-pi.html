Tips and tricks.

Almost all research groups have a departmental or faculty web page. The website usually include basic information about their career, students list, and a list of publications. But can you rely solely on this web page for your lab overall web presence?

  1. You are restricted by the systems and functionality that are permitted on your University website.
  2. Technical upgrades and other structural development of your University website can affect your web presence rankings.
  3. If you switch jobs, you would lose your old website and all the web presence that you acclimated over the years.
  4. Technical support in your university may be limited - both in terms of professionalism and availability.
Academig solves all these problems and much more.
