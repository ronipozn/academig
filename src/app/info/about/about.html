Academig was founded in September of 2018 and based in Germany. Academig is making the life of researchers and research labs simpler. It offers a way to build, manage, discover, follow and compare research labs around the world

Academig connects academic labs all around the world. Whether you are looking to build your lab's profile or find new lab members, projects, services, publications, or positions, Academig offers the easiest way to do exactly that.

Meet the Team

  • Rony Pozner

    Co-Founder, CEO

    Rony is the Chief Executive Officer at Academig. He is responsible for the company's strategy, purpose, and vision. Under Rony's leadership, Academig represents the interests of millions of labs' heads and members around the world. Rony earned a degree in Electrical Engineering from the Technion – Israel Institute of Technology, Master Degree in Nanomaterials from Tel Aviv University and Ph.D. in Quantum Chemistry again at the Technion. He also held several engineering positions before founding Academig. Rony decided to build Academig after becoming frustrated with the cuurent condition of research labs websites and the lack of sufficient accessibility for labs' information.
